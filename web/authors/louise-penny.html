Louise Penny

Canadian author of mystery novels set in the Canadian province of Quebec centred on the work of francophone Chief Inspector Armand Gamache of the Sûreté du Québec.

1958-

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louise_Penny

Notable Quotations from Louise Penny

Four Sentences that Lead to Wisdom

There are four sentences that lead to wisdom. Do with them what you will.

  • I am sorry.
  • I was wrong.
  • I don’t know.
  • I need help.

From the book The Madness of Crowds
